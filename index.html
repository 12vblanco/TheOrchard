Welcome to The Orchard Bar & Restaurant

We are an informal, relaxed bar and restaurant located in Cannonmills, Edinburgh
three minutes walk from the Royal Botanic Gardens

OUR FOOD

At the Orchard Bar & Restaurant we pride ourselves
on using the finest locally sourced fresh produce with an ever changing seasonal menu. Our team of chefs provide a wide range of dishes from traditional pub food to excellent classic meals with a modern touch
Fish & Chips, Haggis Fritters, Homemade Steak Pie and burgers are favourites on our menu. For Vegetarians we have Veggie Burgers, Twice baked goats cheese Soufflé and open sandwich options. Please ask us if you need to see our gluten free menu or have any dietary requirements

We serve food daily, from noon till 8:30pm

OUR DRINKS

We have a wide range of drinks here at the Orchard.
Beers, Wines, Gins, Whiskies, Rums, soft drinks and hot drinks. We have Ales like Deuchars on the pumps plus guest ales that change regularly. We also have a selection of single cask whiskies supplied by Cadenheads which are independently bottled in Campbeltown, for that extra special treat.
Why not try our Highland Park 30 year old cask strength whisky for example or our 8 year old Springbank Sherry cask.
Cadenheads also supply us with our unique cask strength sipping rums. From the FourSquare distillery in Barbados we have an 11 year old and a 12 year old to try, and rums from Belize, Jamaica and Trinidad. Plus old favourites like Havana club from Cuba to Diplomatico from Venezuela.
