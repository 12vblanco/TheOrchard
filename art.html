Exhibitions at The Orchard

In our side room we hold regular art exhibitions across the year supporting local artists and art students to showcase their work. Since we are close to the Botanical Gardens in Edinburgh we encourage natural art, Edinburgh cityscapes, & photography. If you would like to hold an exhibition at The Orchard please email Lorraine at theorchardbar@gmail.com with your details
