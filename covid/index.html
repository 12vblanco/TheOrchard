Dear Customers, We are opening the Orchard for inside drinks and dining, adhering to Covid 19 guidelines which will mean table service only / contactless payments recommended and please book a table in advance as we will have a reduced number of tables to conform with the 1 Metre social distancing rules. If you are showing symptoms of covid or are unwell please do not come in – your temperature might be checked on the way in. We will be operating on lower staff levels so your patience is greatly appreciated at this time. Please note we are accepting bookings for up to 8 persons at this time (and no more than 3 separate households) – depending on seating plans of the day. We will still be operating our take away menu for collection. Please look at our separate Takeaway menu for details (4pm to 8.30pm when our kitchen is open.) Our opening hours are stated in the ‘About Us’ section in this website – our times may change in the future as we progress with the lifting of restrictions.
